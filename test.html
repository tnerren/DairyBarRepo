Property Description

Great opportunity located in high traffic area on Batesville Blvd. in Pleasant Plains, AR!! This was the Pleasant Plains Dairy Bar. Inside features 2 large open spaces for seating and an outside deck complete with tables/seating. Has Fuji Clean septic system, tankless water heater, Extra storage building in back with freezers and shelves. Screened in room in back with Smoker. Plumbing and electrical have been redone. List of equipment available from listing agent. Also includes June Bugs boutique.
